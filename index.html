How to Play Smash Karts

Battle and earn XP

Face off against other players in 3+ minute arena battles for kart-smashing mayhem on multiple maps, with more regularly added. Earn XP from public games to level up, unlocking coins, hats, wheels, and character tokens. Use tokens in the prize machine to get rare new characters.

Pick up weapons

Drive over question mark boxes to pick up random weapons and power-ups like invincibility, missiles, machine guns, and mines. Gear up and cause chaos against opponents in your fully armed kart.

Customize your kart!

Customize your kart and wacky character with a variety of options in the customization menu, including karts, characters, hats, and holiday items. Use in-game coins, character tokens, and gems to purchase upgrades.

Play in events

Experience seasonal events year-round in Smash Karts with new maps and unique rewards during holidays like Halloween and Christmas. Return for fresh festive content and occasions to earn special in-game items.
